Tips for Writing an Essay

  • Use clear and concise language.
  • Avoid jargon and technical terms unless you are sure that your reader will understand them.
  • Use evidence to support your claims.
  • Be objective and unbiased. Avoid expressing your own opinions or beliefs as facts.
  • Use a strong conclusion to summarize your main points and leave the reader with something to think about.
